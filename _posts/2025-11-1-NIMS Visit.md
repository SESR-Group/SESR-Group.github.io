The UCL PI Georgios Nikiforidis gave a talk, “Powering up: Electrolytes In Energy Storage Systems”  as part of the Green Seminar series at the National Institute for Materials Science (NIMS), Japan. The PI host was Dr Shoichi Matsuda. The ongoing collaboration is funded by the UCL global exchange fund.
