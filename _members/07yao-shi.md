Yao received a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Metallurgical Engineering from Central South University. After graduation, he worked as a research assistant at The School of Metallurgy and Environment(SME) at Central South University for one year. Currently, Yao is pursuing a Master's degree in Advanced Materials Science at University College London(UCL).

During his time as a research assistant, Yao focused on the ammonia leaching recovery of lithium-ion battery cathode materials. He co-authored a paper titled "Strengthening the Interfacial Stability of Single-Crystal LiNi₀.₈₈Co₀.₀₉Mn₀.₀₃O₂ Cathode with Multiple-Function Surface Modification," published in Journal of Colloid and Interface Science. Additionally, his undergraduate thesis on "Selective Hydrogen Reduction of Binary Iron-Cobalt Chlorides" was published in Journal of Central South University.
