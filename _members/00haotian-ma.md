Haotian Ma is focusing on data-driven methods to accelerate sustainable energy transitions. He holds a master’s degree from the University of Nottingham and has studied at MIT's Institute of Data, Systems, and Society. Haotian has also conducted significant energy storage simulation research at the University of Birmingham,University of Warwick and the Global Energy Interconnection Research Institute.

He earned his bachelor’s degree in Energy Engineering from Southeast University, where he was recognized as an outstanding graduate in 2019. Haotian has collaborated with industry leaders like the Global Energy Interconnection Research Institute, Huawei European Research Institute, and Nvidia Shanghai, specializing in AI and energy systems modeling.

With over three years of experience, Haotian has optimized energy systems, reduced carbon emissions, and improved efficiency and security through data-driven approaches, contributing to several publications and patents.
